Predict color game

Challenge your observation and prediction skills

Game Introduction

Predict Color Game is a simple and fun puzzle game designed to challenge players' observation and prediction skills. In this game, players need to predict what the next color will be based on the given patterns and hints. The game is divided into multiple levels, and the difficulty of each level gradually increases, allowing players to improve their skills through continuous challenges.

Game Rules

At the start of each level, players will see a pattern of colored squares, each representing a specific color. The player needs to observe the pattern and predict what color will come next based on the hints given. The prompt can be a pattern related to the colors of the previous blocks, such as "repeat", "alternate", "increment", etc. After the player selects the predicted color, they submit their answer. If the answer is correct, the player will advance to the next more challenging level; if the answer is wrong, the player will lose a chance, but can try again.

Game Features

Simple and easy to use

The game rules are simple and clear, suitable for players of all ages.

intellectual challenge

By observing and analyzing patterns, players can exercise their logical thinking and prediction skills.

Diverse levels

The game is designed with multiple levels, each with different challenges and rules to ensure that players will not feel bored in the game.

Customizability

Players can choose different game modes and difficulties according to their preferences, as well as custom modes.
