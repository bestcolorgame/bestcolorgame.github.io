What is Andar Bahar?

Andar Bahar is a game of pure chance that involves two main bets, Andar (inside) and Bahar (outside), with cards dealt from a single deck. The game starts with a dealer placing one card face-up and players betting on whether a card with the same face value will appear on Andar or Bahar side. The simplicity and quick nature of the game make it incredibly popular among gaming enthusiasts.
