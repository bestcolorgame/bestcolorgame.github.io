Introduction to Color Games

Color games are not just fun—they also test your color perception and decision-making skills under pressure.

General Tips for Improving at Color Games

Here are some universal tips that apply to most color-based challenges.

  • Practice Color Differentiation: Spend time daily on apps designed to improve your ability to distinguish colors.
  • Adjust Your Display Settings: Ensure your screen is correctly calibrated for color accuracy.
  • Play in Well-Lit Environments: Good lighting can help prevent eye strain and make colors appear clearer.

Tips for Specific Color Games

Color Switch

For Color Switch, timing and pattern recognition are key. Watching the patterns and predicting the next color can save you from making a mistake.

Blendoku

With Blendoku, your approach should be methodical. Sort colors in your mind before placing them, considering both hue and saturation.

Conclusion

Whether you are a casual player or aiming to master color games, these tips will help you enhance your gameplay and possibly your cognitive abilities in color perception.
