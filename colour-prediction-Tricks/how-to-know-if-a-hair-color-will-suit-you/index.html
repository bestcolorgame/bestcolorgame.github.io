If it’s hard to determine the vein colors, you likely have a neutral undertone. - **For cool undertones:** Opt for hair colors with ashy, silver, or platinum notes. - **For warm undertones:** Go for shades like gold, caramel, honey, or rich browns. - **For neutral undertones:** You're in luck! Natural blondes might easily transition to different shades of blonde and light brown.
