Cool Tricks to Enhance Your Daily Life

1. Master the Art of Speed Reading

Speed reading is a fantastic skill that can save you hours of time, especially if you're a student or need to sift through large amounts of information daily. Tools like Spreeder and ReadSpeeder can help you develop this skill effectively.

2. Learn Quick Mental Math Tricks

Being able to perform math quickly in your head can impress friends and make daily decisions easier. Learn tricks like multiplying large numbers using simple geometry or using the rule of 72 to estimate investment growth.

3. DIY Household Hacks

DIY tricks can turn household chores into a breeze. For example, use vinegar and baking soda to create a powerful cleaning agent or learn to fold clothes in seconds using cardboard cutouts.

4. Enhance Your Memory with Mnemonics

Memory can be significantly improved with practices such as mnemonics. For instance, use acronyms or vivid imagery to remember complex information like the planets in our solar system or elements on the periodic table.

5. Smartphone Photography Tricks

Improve your smartphone photography skills with tools and tricks that professionals use. For example, use the grid function for better composition, or experiment with different manual settings to enhance the mood of your photos.
