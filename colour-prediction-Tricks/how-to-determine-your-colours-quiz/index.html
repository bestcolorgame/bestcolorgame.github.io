Discover Your Best Colors with Our Quiz

Finding the right colors that enhance your appearance can boost your confidence and make your outfit choices simpler. Whether you want to refine your wardrobe, choose colors for your brand, or look fabulous for a special event, understanding your personal color palette is key. Below is a guide on how to determine your colors through a simple quiz.

Step 1: Identify Your Skin Tone

Knowing your skin's undertone is the first step in finding your ideal colors. Skin undertones are categorized into three types: warm, cool, and neutral.

  • Warm: Your skin has hints of yellow, peachy or golden hues.
  • Cool: Your skin has hints of pink, red, or bluish hues.
  • Neutral: Your skin does not have any obvious pink, red, blue, or yellow undertones, or it may be a mix of both.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair and Eye Color

Your natural hair and eye colors can help determine which colors complement you the most. Typically, certain palettes enhance natural hair and eye colors significantly.

Step 3: Take the Quiz

Answer these questions to get a better idea of your color profile:

  1. What is the natural color of your hair?
    • Blonde
    • Brown
    • Black
    • Red
    • Grey
  2. What is the color of your eyes?
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Brown
    • Hazel
  3. How would you describe your skin's undertone?
    • Warm (yellow, peachy, golden)
    • Cool (pink, red, bluish)
    • Neutral (no obvious overtones, or a mixture of both)
  4. How does your skin react to sun exposure?
    • Easily burns, rarely tans
    • Tans gradually and burns occasionally
    • Easily tans, rarely burns

Step 4: Analyze Your Answers

If your features align more with warm shades, you might look best in earth tones like red, orange, yellow, and olive green. For cooler undertones, vibrant shades like blue, purple, and emerald tend to enhance your look. Neutrals might look great in both color universes but will shine in tailored beige, greys, and whites.
