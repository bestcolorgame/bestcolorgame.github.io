Understanding 1X2 Betting

1X2 betting, also known as three-way betting, refers to backing or laying a home win, draw, or away win. This is typically used in soccer to predict the outcome of a match. The ‘1’ represents a home win, the ‘X’ stands for a draw, and the ‘2’ signifies an away win.
