Discover Your Color Season

Understanding your color season can transform the way you dress and help you look your best. By determining whether you are a Spring, Summer, Autumn, or Winter, you can select clothing, makeup, and accessories that complement your natural tones.

What is a Color Season?

Color seasons are based on a theory that associates your skin tone, eye color, and hair color with a specific palette of colors that harmonizes naturally with your complexion. Each season represents a set of colors that are best suited to individuals with specific color profiles.

How to Find Your Color Season

Here's a simple quiz to help you determine your color season. Analyze your features according to the guidelines below and match them with the corresponding season:

Step 1: Look at Your Hair Color

  • Spring: Golden blonde, strawberry blonde, light auburn
  • Summer: Ashy blonde, silver or ashy brown, soft brunette
  • Autumn: Warm brown, rich auburn, warm black
  • Winter: Deep brown, stark black, cool-toned dark brown

Step 2: Examine Your Skin Tone

  • Spring: Creamy white or peach
  • Summer: Pale pink, softly beige, cool undertones
  • Autumn: Golden beige, warm undertones often with freckles
  • Winter: Clear white, olive, or cool brown

Step 3: Consider Your Eye Color

  • Spring: Light blue, green, light hazel
  • Summer: Soft blue, gray-blue, soft hazel
  • Autumn: Dark hazel, spiced brown
  • Winter: Deep blue, charcoal gray, dark brown

Step 4: Overall Vibe

Identify the overall vibe of your features: Is it soft and muted, or clear and vibrant? Springs and Autumns tend to have a warmer, golden quality to their complexions, while Summers and Winters feature cooler tones.

Tips to Use Your Color Palette

Once you've identified your color season, use these tips to integrate it into your fashion choices:

  • Clothing: Choose clothes that align with your color palette for a naturally harmonious look.
  • Makeup: Use foundation, blush, and lip colors that complement your season to enhance your natural beauty.
  • Accessories: Opt for jewelry and other accessories that resonate with your season's characteristics.

Remember, the most important thing is that you feel confident and comfortable in your colors. Experiment within your color palette, and have fun expressing your unique style!
