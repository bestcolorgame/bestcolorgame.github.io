Understanding Andar Bahar

Andar Bahar is traditionally played with a single deck of cards. The game starts with the dealer placing one card face up. This is called the 'Joker' or 'Game Card'. The goal is to predict whether a card with the same rank will appear on the 'Andar' (inside) or 'Bahar' (outside) side. Players place their bets based on their predictions.
