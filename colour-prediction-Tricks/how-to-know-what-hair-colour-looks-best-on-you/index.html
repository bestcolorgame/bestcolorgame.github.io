Knowing this can help you choose a hair color that complements or contrasts effectively. - **Warm skin tones:** Look best with similarly warm hair colors like golden blonde, rich chocolate browns, or auburn reds. - **Cool skin tones:** Best suited to cooler colors such as platinum, ash blonde, ash brown, or cool dark browns. - **Neutral skin tones:** Can wear a wide range of colors but often look best with very natural shades. ### 2. **Consider Your Eye Color** Hair color can make your eye color pop: - **Blue eyes:** Golden blondes or rich brown tones can highlight blue eyes strikingly. - **Green eyes:** Shades of red or brown can enhance the green in your eyes. - **Brown eyes:** Almost any hair color suits brown eyes, but deep tones can add mystery, while light tones can soften your features. ### 3. **Look at Your Natural Hair Color** Building on the natural tones in your hair can give a more seamless, flattering result: - If your hair is naturally light, going too dark might be jarring, but subtle dark shades can add depth. - If your hair is naturally dark, consider how lightening it might affect its health but highlighting can add dimension without extensive damage. ### 4. **Experiment with Temporary Colors** Before committing to a permanent change, try hair color waxes or wash-in/wash-out colors. These can give you an idea of how a color might look on you without the long-term commitment. ### 5. **Professional Consultation** A professional colorist can provide expert advice tailored to your features and preferences. They can also ensure that your hair remains healthy and vibrant post-coloring. ### 6. **Use Technology** Many hair color brands offer virtual try-ons through their websites or apps. Upload your photo to see how different colors look on you from the comfort of your home. ### 7. **Consider Maintenance and Upkeep** Some colors require more maintenance than others. Lighter colors might require frequent root touch-ups; vivid colors might fade quickly and necessitate regular appointments. Choose a color you can realistically maintain considering your lifestyle. ### 8. **Reflect on Your Wardrobe and Makeup** Think about the colors you typically wear. Certain hair colors might clash with your usual palette, while others could enhance it. Also, changing your hair color might mean adjusting your makeup shades.
