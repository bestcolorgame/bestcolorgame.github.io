Why Color Matters

Colors can influence our mood, perceptions, and interactions. Wearing colors that complement your skin tone, hair color, and eyes can make you look more vibrant and feel more positive. For example, the right colors can brighten your face and make you look more awake and energetic, while the wrong colors can make you look tired or washed out.

How the Quiz Works

You'll answer a series of questions about your physical characteristics and personal preferences, and the quiz will suggest which season you likely belong to, guiding you toward the colors that suit you best.
