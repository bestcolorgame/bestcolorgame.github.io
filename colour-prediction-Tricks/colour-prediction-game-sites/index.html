What are Colour Prediction Games?

Colour prediction games are a form of online betting where players try to predict which color will appear in a sequence or as a result of a game mechanism. These games, which have gained immense popularity worldwide, typically feature a simple, fast-paced format that makes them accessible and entertaining. The allure of quick results and potential rewards keeps players coming back for more.

How Do These Games Work?

The typical colour prediction game operates on a random output generated by software algorithms. Players choose a color from a set (commonly two or three options like red, green, and violet) before a timer runs out. After bets are placed, the game reveals the color, and winners receive their winnings based on payout rates specific to the game.
