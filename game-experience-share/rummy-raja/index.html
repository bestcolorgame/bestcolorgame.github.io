The Exciting game of Rummy Raja

Rummy Raja is a popular card game that has been enjoyed by people of all ages for many years. It is a game of skill, strategy, and luck that can be played with 2-6 players. The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards and the objective is to form sets and sequences of cards before your opponents do.

Rules of Rummy Raja:

1. Each player is dealt 13 cards at the beginning of the game.

2. The remaining cards are placed face down on the table to form the draw pile.

3. The top card from the draw pile is placed face up to start the discard pile.

4. A player's turn consists of drawing a card from the draw pile or the discard pile and then discarding a card.

5. The objective is to form runs (sequences of consecutive cards in the same suit) and sets (three or four cards of the same rank).

6. Once a player has formed all of their cards into valid sets and runs, they can declare and end the game.

7. The other players must then total up the value of the cards left in their hands and add them to their score.

8. The player with the lowest score at the end of the game is declared the winner.

Strategy in Rummy Raja:

1. Pay attention to what cards your opponents are picking up and discarding to try and figure out what sets and runs they are trying to form.

2. Keep track of the cards that have been discarded to help you decide which cards to pick up and which to discard.

3. Try to form your sets and runs as quickly as possible to avoid getting caught with high-value cards in your hand.

4. Be strategic in your discards to try and throw off your opponents and prevent them from forming their sets and runs.

5. Don't be afraid to rearrange your sets and runs during the game if it will help you form new sets and runs more quickly.

Rummy Raja is a game that requires both skill and strategy to win. It is a fun and exciting game that can be enjoyed by players of all ages. So gather your friends and family and try your hand at Rummy Raja today!
