India's most popular color game

Welcome to the Colour Prediction Game!

Are you ready to put your colour knowledge to the test? This fun and interactive game will challenge your ability to predict various colours and see if you can identify them correctly. Let's jump right in!

How to Play:

  1. You will be presented with a colour swatch.
  2. Before the colour is revealed, try to predict what colour it will be.
  3. Once you've made your prediction, the colour will be revealed.
  4. See if your prediction was correct!
  5. Keep playing to test your skills and see how many colours you can predict accurately.
