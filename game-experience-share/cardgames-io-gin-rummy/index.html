How to Play Gin Rummy

Gin Rummy is typically played with 2 players and a standard deck of 52 cards. The objective of the game is to form sets and runs of cards in your hand, with the ultimate goal of going "gin" by getting rid of all your cards. Players take turns drawing and discarding cards until one player achieves the goal and ends the round.
