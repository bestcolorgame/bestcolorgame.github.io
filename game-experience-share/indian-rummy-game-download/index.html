What is Indian Rummy?

Indian Rummy is a variation of the classic rummy game that is played in India. The game is usually played with two decks of cards and two jokers. The objective of the game is to form sets and sequences of cards and make a valid declaration before your opponents do. The game is fast-paced, strategic, and incredibly fun to play.
