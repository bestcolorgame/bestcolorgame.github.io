Rummy Card Games List

Rummy is a popular card game that is widely played around the world. There are many variations of the game, each with its own set of rules and gameplay. If you're looking to try out different types of rummy card games, here is a list of some of the most popular ones:

1. Gin Rummy

Gin Rummy is a classic variation of rummy that is played with two players. The objective of the game is to form sets and runs of cards in your hand. The game is typically played over several rounds, with the winner being the player with the lowest total points at the end of the game.

2. Indian Rummy

Indian Rummy is a popular variation of rummy that is commonly played in India. The game is typically played with two decks of cards and two jokers. The objective of the game is to form sequences and sets of cards in your hand. Indian Rummy can be played with 2 to 6 players.

3. Kalooki Rummy

Kalooki Rummy is a variation of rummy that is popular in the Caribbean. The game is typically played with two to four players and two decks of cards. The objective of the game is to form sets and runs of cards in your hand. Kalooki Rummy is often played in tournament settings.

4. Canasta

Canasta is a rummy-like card game that is typically played with four players in two partnerships. The game is played with two decks of cards and the objective is to form melds of seven cards of the same rank. Canasta has many variations, including Classic Canasta and Hand and Foot Canasta.

These are just a few examples of the many rummy card games that are out there. Whether you're a seasoned rummy player or new to the game, there is a rummy variation that is sure to suit your preferences. So gather your friends and family, shuffle up a deck of cards, and enjoy a fun game of rummy!
