What is Ramya Game?

Ramya Game is a popular board game that originated in India. It is a strategy game that involves moving pieces around a board to capture your opponent's pieces. The game is played with two players, each controlling their set of pieces, which are typically different in color to differentiate between the two sides.

How to Play Ramya Game

The objective of the game is to capture all of your opponent's pieces or to block them in such a way that they cannot make any moves. Players take turns moving their pieces diagonally across the board, capturing their opponent's pieces by jumping over them. The player who captures all of their opponent's pieces first wins the game.
