Mantri Mall Colour Prediction

Mantri Mall in Bangalore is one of the most popular shopping destinations in the city, known for its wide range of stores and vibrant atmosphere. One unique feature of the mall is its Colour Prediction game, which has been a hit among visitors.

The Colour Prediction game at Mantri Mall is a fun and interactive activity where participants can guess the next colour that will appear on a spinning wheel. Players can place their bets on a specific colour, and if the wheel lands on their chosen colour, they win exciting prizes.

The game is easy to play and appeals to people of all ages. It adds an element of excitement to the shopping experience at Mantri Mall and has become a popular attraction for both locals and tourists.

How to Play the Colour Prediction Game:

  1. Visit the designated area for the Colour Prediction game at Mantri Mall.
  2. Place your bet on the colour you think will appear next on the spinning wheel.
  3. Wait for the wheel to stop spinning to see if your prediction was correct.
  4. If the wheel lands on your chosen colour, you win a prize!

Benefits of Playing the Colour Prediction Game at Mantri Mall:

1. Entertainment: The game provides entertainment and excitement for visitors, adding to the overall shopping experience at Mantri Mall.

2. Prizes: Participants have the chance to win exciting prizes, making the game even more enjoyable.

3. Social Interaction: Playing the game with friends and family can be a fun way to bond and create lasting memories.
