How to Play

In a typical colour prediction game, players are presented with a grid of coloured squares or shapes. The objective is to predict the next colour that will appear in the sequence. Players can place their bets on different colours and wait for the result. If their prediction is correct, they win! It's a simple yet engaging gameplay that can keep you entertained for hours.
