How to Play Ultimate Rummy Free

The objective of the game is to form sets or runs of cards in your hand. A set is a group of three or four cards with the same rank but different suits, while a run is a sequence of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit. The first player to form valid sets and runs wins the game.
