What is Indian Rummy?

Indian Rummy, also known as 'Paplu', is a card game that is a variation of the original Rummy game. The game is played with two decks of cards and two jokers. The objective of the game is to form sequences and sets using the cards that are dealt to each player. The player who successfully melds all their cards first wins the game.
