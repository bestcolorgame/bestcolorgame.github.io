How to Play Rummy Pay

Rummy Pay follows the traditional rules of the card game Rummy. The objective of the game is to form sets and sequences of cards before your opponents. Each player is dealt a certain number of cards, and they must form valid sets (groups of cards with the same rank but different suits) and sequences (consecutive cards of the same suit).

Players take turns drawing and discarding cards until a player can form valid sets and sequences to declare. Once a player declares, the remaining players have to count the points in their hand, based on the cards they were unable to meld, and the player with the least points wins the round.
