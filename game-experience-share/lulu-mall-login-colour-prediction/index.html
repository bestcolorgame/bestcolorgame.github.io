Lulu Mall Login Colour Prediction

Lulu Mall, one of the largest shopping malls in India, has recently introduced a new feature called "Colour Prediction" in their login page. This feature aims to make the login process more interactive and engaging for users.

When users visit the Lulu Mall website and navigate to the login page, they are greeted with a colour prediction game. Users are presented with a grid of colourful options and are asked to predict the next colour in the sequence. This fun and interactive game not only adds an element of excitement to the login process but also helps to enhance user experience.

By successfully predicting the next colour in the sequence, users are able to proceed with the login process and access their accounts. This innovative approach to login authentication not only adds a layer of security but also makes the entire experience more enjoyable for users.

The Colour Prediction feature at Lulu Mall has received positive feedback from users who appreciate the creativity and uniqueness it brings to the login process. It not only sets Lulu Mall apart from other shopping malls but also showcases their commitment to enhancing user experience through innovation.
