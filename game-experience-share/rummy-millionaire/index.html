How to Play Rummy

Rummy is a card game that is played with a standard deck of 52 cards. The goal of the game is to form sets and sequences of cards. A set is three or four cards of the same rank but different suits, while a sequence is three or more cards of the same suit in consecutive order.

Players are dealt a certain number of cards at the beginning of the game and must form valid sets and sequences to declare a win. Rummy requires a combination of skill, strategy, and quick thinking to outsmart your opponents.
