How to Play Color Switch:

The rules of Color Switch are straightforward. You control a ball that can only pass through obstacles that match its color. Tap the screen to make the ball jump, and time your jumps carefully to avoid colliding with the wrong color. Collect stars along the way to unlock new balls and themes.
