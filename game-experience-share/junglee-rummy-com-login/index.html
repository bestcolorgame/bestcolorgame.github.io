Step 2: Click on the "Login" Button

Once you are on the Junglee Rummy website, click on the "Login" button located at the top right corner of the homepage.

Enter your registered email address and password in the respective fields. Make sure to enter the correct details to access your account.

Once you have successfully logged in, you can start playing rummy games, join tournaments, and explore various promotions and offers available on Junglee Rummy.
