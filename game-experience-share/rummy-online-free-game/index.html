How to Play Rummy Online

To play Rummy online, you can either download Rummy game apps on your mobile device or play on websites that offer the game. The rules of online Rummy are the same as traditional Rummy. The objective of the game is to form sets or sequences of cards before your opponents.

Players are dealt a hand of cards, and they need to draw and discard cards to form melds. A meld can be a set of three or four cards of the same rank or a sequence of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit. The game continues until a player successfully melds all their cards.
