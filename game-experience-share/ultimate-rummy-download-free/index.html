What is Ultimate Rummy?

How to Play Ultimate Rummy?

Playing Ultimate Rummy is simple. The goal of the game is to form sets or runs of cards in your hand. A set is a group of three or four cards of the same rank but different suits, while a run is a group of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit. The player who successfully forms these sets or runs first wins the game.

Each player is dealt a certain number of cards, and the remaining cards form the draw pile. Players take turns to draw a card from either the draw pile or the discard pile and then discard a card to the discard pile. The game continues until a player successfully declares by forming valid sets or runs.
