Rules of Rummy 500

Just like the traditional Rummy game, Rummy 500 is played with a standard deck of 52 cards. The objective of the game is to form sets and runs of cards in your hand. A set consists of three or four cards of the same rank, while a run consists of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit.
