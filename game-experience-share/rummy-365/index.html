The Basics of Rummy

Rummy is a card game that is played with two decks of cards and two jokers. The objective of the game is to form sets and sequences of cards before your opponents. A set is a group of three or four cards of the same rank but different suits, while a sequence is a group of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit.
