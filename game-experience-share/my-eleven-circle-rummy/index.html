The Exciting Game of Eleven Circle Rummy

Eleven Circle Rummy is a fun and engaging card game that combines elements of rummy with a unique twist. In this game, players aim to form sets and sequences of cards to score points and ultimately win the game. The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards and is suitable for 2-6 players.

Rules of the Game:

  1. Each player is dealt 11 cards at the beginning of the game.
  2. The remaining cards are placed face down to form the draw pile.
  3. Players take turns to either draw a card from the draw pile or the discard pile.
  4. The objective is to form sets or sequences of cards in your hand.
  5. A set is formed by having three or four cards of the same rank but different suits.
  6. A sequence is formed by having three or more consecutive cards of the same suit.
  7. Once a player has formed a valid set or sequence, they can place it on the table.
  8. The game continues until a player has formed sets or sequences with all their cards.
  9. The remaining players count the points in their hands, with face cards worth 10 points and numbered cards worth their face value.
  10. The player with the lowest total points wins the game.

Tips for Winning:

  • Focus on forming sequences as they are worth more points.
  • Keep track of the cards your opponents pick and discard to guess their possible sets or sequences.
  • Discard high-value cards early in the game to reduce your total points.
  • Pay attention to the cards you pick and aim to create multiple sets or sequences in one turn.
  • Be strategic in your moves and try to block your opponents from completing their sets or sequences.
