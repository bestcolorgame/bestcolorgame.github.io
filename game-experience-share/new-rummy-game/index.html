How to Play Rummy Master:

Playing Rummy Master is easy and enjoyable. Simply follow these basic rules to get started:

  1. First, arrange the cards in your hand by grouping them into sets and sequences.
  2. Draw a card from the deck or the discard pile to complete your sets/sequences.
  3. Discard a card that you don't need to end your turn.
  4. The goal is to form valid sets/sequences and declare your hand before your opponents do.
