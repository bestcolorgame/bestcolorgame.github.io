How to Play Online Rummy

In online Rummy, players are dealt a hand of cards and the objective is to form sets and sequences with the cards in hand. The game is typically played with 2 decks of cards and 2 jokers. Players need to form a pure sequence (a sequence without a joker) and an impure sequence (a sequence with a joker) along with sets to declare and win the game.
