How to Play Net Rummy

Net Rummy is typically played with two decks of cards and two jokers. The objective of the game is to form valid sets and sequences from the 13 cards that each player is dealt. A valid set consists of three or four cards of the same rank but different suits, while a valid sequence comprises of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit.

Players take turns to draw a card from the closed or open deck and discard one card to reduce their hand to the required 13 cards. The player who successfully melds all their cards into valid sets and sequences first wins the game.
