Rummy: All Games List

Rummy is a popular card game played by people of all ages around the world. There are several variations of the game, each with its own set of rules and strategies. Here is a list of some of the most popular rummy games:

1. Indian Rummy

Indian Rummy is a popular variant of rummy that is widely played in India. It is usually played with two deck of cards and two jokers. The game is played with 2-6 players and each player is dealt 13 cards. The objective of the game is to form sequences and sets with the cards in hand.

2. Gin Rummy

Gin Rummy is a two-player card game that is derived from the original rummy game. The game is played with a standard deck of 52 cards, and the objective is to form sets and runs to score points. The game is usually played to a certain number of points, and the player who reaches that score first wins the game.

3. Rummy 500

Rummy 500 is a variation of rummy that is played with a standard deck of 52 cards. The game is played with 2-8 players, and each player is dealt a hand of 13 cards. The objective of the game is to reach 500 points by forming sets and runs with the cards in hand. The player who reaches 500 points first wins the game.

4. Kalooki (Kaluki)

Kalooki, also known as Kaluki, is a version of rummy that is played with wild cards. The game is usually played with 2-4 players, and the objective is to form sets and runs with the cards in hand. The game is played to a certain number of points, and the player who reaches that score first wins the game.

5. Canasta

Canasta is a rummy game that is typically played with four players in partnerships. The game is played with two deck of cards and the objective is to form melds of seven cards of the same rank. Canasta has its own unique set of rules and scoring system, making it a challenging and dynamic rummy variant.

These are just a few of the many rummy games that are played around the world. Each game has its own set of rules and strategies, making rummy a diverse and engaging card game for players of all skill levels.
