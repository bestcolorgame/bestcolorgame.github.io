Indian Rummy

Indian Rummy is a variation of the popular card game rummy. It is a skill-based game that is easy to learn but takes time to master. The game is typically played with two decks of cards and two jokers. The objective of Indian Rummy is to form sequences and sets using the cards in your hand.
