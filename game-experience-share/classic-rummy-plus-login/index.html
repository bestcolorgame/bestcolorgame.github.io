How to Play

Classic Rummy Plus follows the same basic rules as traditional rummy. The objective of the game is to form sets and sequences of cards in your hand. A set is a group of three or four cards of the same rank but different suits, while a sequence is a group of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit.

Players take turns drawing and discarding cards in an attempt to form valid sets and sequences. The game ends when a player successfully melds all of their cards into sets and sequences, or when the draw pile runs out of cards.
